Welcome

notedown lets you take down notes.

fast and beautiful.




At the heart of notedown lies the simple idea - one-key shortcuts activated by space.

Shortcut keys are checked only as the first key in the line, so you decide formatting of line first, type a space and voila, start typing beautifully! The formatting gets deactivated on next line. So next time you take down notes, type faster than normal giving every detail to formatting.
