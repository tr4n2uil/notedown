Welcome

notedown lets you take down notes.

fast and beautiful.




At the heart of notedown lies the simple idea - one-key shortcuts activated by space.

Shortcut keys are checked only as the first key in the line, so you decide formatting of line first, type a space and voila, start typing beautifully! The formatting gets deactivated on next line. So next time you take down notes, type faster than normal giving every detail to formatting.

Available Shortcuts:

KeyShortcut for
bbold
iitalic
uunderline
*unordered list
+ordered list
tcheck box

Notedown allows you to send snapshots over email. Simply click on the to copy the notes into clipboard and open compose email dialog. Enter your email id and paste your notes into the body and send.

Privacy

notedown uses HTML5 localStorage.


That means all your data are stored within your browser.

No data is sent to servers. We care for our users privacy :)
